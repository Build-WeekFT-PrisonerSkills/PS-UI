FOR EMPLOYERS FOR PRISONS

Own a business? Want to do good in the world? Look no further! "Second Chance" is an intuitive web platform that allows you to look into different prison demographics near you and analyze the different individual resumes and skill sets as potential employees for your business.


Some inmates have permissions to work away from prison and others are close to transitioning back into civilian life so they can become permanent assets to your team if you desire!

OUR MISSION

We believe everyone deserves an opportunity to start over with a clean slate, so we created Second Chance to give people a future that doesn't have to be defined by their past.
