About Us

Here at Second Chance, we are a passionate team of developers and students at Lambda School! We are very passionate about coding, programming, engineering, and the like, as well as helping those around us. We recognize each life is of equal value and while life often takes us on different journeys, this does not mean that there is not a wonderful destination at the end. We all decided that we would like to help former inmates make their transition back into society and help them reach their "destination". We all worked very hard and are very proud of our project. Please reach out with questions!
