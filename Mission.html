Our Mission:

Our Mission here at Second Chance is to change the lives of those who are less fortunate and vulnerable. We belive we can do this by partnering with inmates, former inmates, prison staff, and employers to increase job placements for those inmates and those transitioning back into society. It is also our mission that we make sure these individuals are fully prepared for the workforce and that thay are an asset to their new employers.
